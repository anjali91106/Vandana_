About Vandana

Vandana is a non-profit initiative dedicated to making quality education accessible to every child in India, focusing on local, school-based curriculum. We believe in the power of technology to bridge gaps and empower both students and teachers. Our platform is designed to be child-friendly, engaging, and a powerful tool for tracking and encouraging academic progress, truly embodying the spirit of "Sikhshit Bharat, Viksit Bharat."
